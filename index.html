Letter from the Secretary Generals,
GIMUN’24

Dear Citizens of the World, I am pleased to welcome all of you to GIMUN 24 at Galgotias College and hope that you approach this conference with an open mind, a curious spirit and a willingness to question conventional wisdom.
Model United Nations, as a concept, for me has always been associated with challenging thoughts and proposing innovative solutions to complex global problems.
The theme 'QUAM ARTIBUS: BEYOND DIPLOMACY' aims to explore the lasting impact of historical events on today's grave challenges. It encourages innovative solutions beyond traditional diplomatic approaches, fostering insightful discourse and pragmatic resolutions. It is my belief that by exploring new possibilities and considering the long-term impact of our solutions, we can create a better future for all.
Through your participation in this conference, you have the opportunity to become a key agent for change and innovation. As we embark on this conference with Galgotias Student Council leading the proceedings, let us do so with respect and openmindedness I wish you all a productive and rewarding experience!

~ Ayush Agarwal & Himanshu Mishra
